Curry

Description

This is a delicious curry.

I like curry because it is made with coconut milk.

I love coconut milk.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Salted butter
  • Coconut Milk
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cube all the pork.
  2. Open the cans of coconut milk.
  3. Pour into a crock pot.
  4. Cook on high heat for three hours.
  5. Enjoy!
