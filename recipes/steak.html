Steak

Description

This is a delicious steak recipe. I use it to make all my steaks.

Steak is very good for you because it contains protein.

This is a good thing, especially if you are over 40.

Ingredients

  • A steak
  • Salted butter
  • Low-sodium soy sauce
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Marinate the steak in the soy sauce and spices.
  2. Set the oven to 450 degrees fahrenheit.
  3. Fry the steak in butter on both sides.
  4. Place steak on glass baking tray and bake for nine minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
